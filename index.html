Ramadan Favorite

Chebakia

Chebakia is a flower-shaped Moroccan cookie fried until crisp, then coated in honey and sprinkled with sesame seeds. It is especially popular during Ramadan and is one of the most recognizable Moroccan sweets.

Elegant Pastry

Kaab El Ghazal

Known as gazelle horns, this pastry has a delicate outer shell with a fragrant almond filling flavored with orange blossom water. It is one of the most refined and elegant desserts in Moroccan baking.

Tea Time Classic

Fekkas

Fekkas is a crunchy twice-baked Moroccan biscuit often made with almonds, raisins, sesame, or chocolate. It is perfect with tea or coffee and gives the page a bakery display feel.

Street Sweet

Sfenj

Sfenj is a Moroccan donut with a crisp outside and airy inside. It is often enjoyed warm, plain, or lightly sweetened, and is one of the most comforting traditional treats.

Honey Pastry

Briouat

Briouat is a crisp triangular pastry usually filled with sweet almond paste, fried until golden, and glazed with honey. It is rich, festive, and very common on Moroccan dessert trays.

Roasted Sweet

Sellou (Sfouf)

Sellou is made from toasted flour, almonds, sesame, butter, and warm spices. It has a rich crumbly texture and is deeply connected with Ramadan and celebration tables.

Classic Cookie

Ghriba Bahla

Ghriba Bahla is a cracked Moroccan cookie with a rustic look and soft texture. It is simple but loved for its buttery feel and homemade bakery charm.

Coconut Sweet

Ghriba Coconut

This coconut version of ghriba has a sweet, tender, and slightly chewy center. It brings a softer bakery-style variation to traditional Moroccan cookies.

Almond Cookie

Ghriba Almond

Ghriba Almond is a soft cookie made with ground almonds and sugar. It is delicate, rich, and one of the most popular sweets served with Moroccan tea.

Date Filled

Makrout

Makrout is a semolina pastry filled with date paste, then baked or fried and coated in honey. Its rich filling and grainy exterior make it especially satisfying.

Honey Pancake

Baghrir

Baghrir is known as the thousand-hole pancake because of its spongy surface. It is often served warm with melted butter and honey for a soft and comforting sweet dish.

Layered Favorite

Msemen with Honey

Msemen is a flaky layered pancake or flatbread. Sweetened with honey, it becomes a rich breakfast or dessert with a buttery texture and beautiful folded layers.

Semolina Cake

Harcha Sweet

Sweet harcha is a semolina griddle cake with a golden crust and crumbly inside. It is simple, warm, and perfect with honey, butter, or jam.

Luxury Dessert

Jawhara

Jawhara is a royal dessert made with crisp pastry layers, cream filling, and almond garnish. It feels elegant, rich, and more dramatic than an everyday cookie display.

Syrup Spiral

Zlabia

Zlabia is a bright, glossy fried sweet soaked in syrup or honey. Its spiral look makes it visually striking and perfect for a flashier dessert gallery.

Sweet Bread

Krachel

Krachel is a soft Moroccan brioche flavored with anise, sesame, and orange blossom. It is light, fragrant, and works well in a bakery-style layout.
